Summary

The Informational Earth-Likeness Score (IELS) measures how closely an informational system resembles Earth-like balance in stability, regeneration, coherence, and entropy management. 

IELS = (S × R × C × E)1/4

Systems with IELS near 1.0 exhibit Earth-like informational harmony.

Verification Method

The script simulates a synthetic informational ecosystem and computes normalized indicators:

  • S — Stability
  • R — Regeneration
  • C — Coherence
  • E — Entropy-balance

These indicators are combined into the IELS score using the geometric mean. 

IELS = (S_mean * R_mean * C_mean * E_mean)**0.25
